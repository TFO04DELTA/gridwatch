Honesty checklist

• One day is noise; weeks are evidence.
• Weather flags: trailing-3h gusts/wind/precip/temp + NWS alerts + utility-admitted weather causes.
• Operating DCs are transmission-interconnected; the plausible mechanism is construction-phase distribution stress.
• Reliability ≠ rates. PJM capacity costs are a separate dataset.
